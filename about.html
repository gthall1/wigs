About

Wig's Wheels

Patrick Wigley, known by most as Wig, a nickname acquired during his stint as a US Army medic, opened Wig’s Wheels in downtown Anniston in 2005. With over 20 years of experience spinning wrenches we have learned a thing or two about bicycles. We will service any brand of bike. When you bring your bike in for service we will give you an estimate for the work that’s needed. Appointments are not necessary but helpful. We know how important every ride is, and if we can fix it on the spot we will. We stock most replacement parts so that we can have you back rolling as quickly as possible. Our mechanic is a certified USA Cycling Race Mechanic, License #9447.

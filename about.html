About

Wig's Wheels

Patrick Wigley, known by most as Wig, a nickname acquired during his stint as a US Army medic, opened Wig’s Wheels in downtown Anniston in 2005. He is a USA Cycling Certified Race Mechanic and a passionate proponent of all things cycling. He has been actively involved in the Chief Ladiga Trail and Coldwater Mountain Bike trail expansions.
